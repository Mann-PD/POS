**Software Requirements Specification (SRS)**

**Fruit Point of Sale (POS) System**

The Fruit POS System is a digital billing solution designed for fruit
shops and small fruit businesses. It replaces manual billing and
calculations with a fast and easy system that supports selling fruits by
both weight and quantity. Employees can quickly select items, generate
bills, and complete sales with automatic price calculations that reduce
errors. All sales are recorded in real time, and stock levels are
updated instantly. Shop owners can easily manage daily fruit prices,
inventory, staff access, and sales reports through role-based control.
Overall, the Fruit POS System helps make shop operations faster, more
accurate, and more reliable.
